TempMail - Temporary Email Service

Welcome to TempMail, a Python-based program to generate secure, disposable email addresses.
Your personal email remains confidential while utilizing the TempMail inbox for sign-ups, verifications.
Your temporary inbox will remain active for the duration of your session, after which all emails will be automatically deleted upon closing the program.
TempMail ensures complete privacy and convenience during your use of the service.
Thank you for choosing TempMail!
