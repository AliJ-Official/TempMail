TempMail - Temporary Email Service

Welcome to TempMail, your secure temporary email solution!
This program provides you with an instant disposable email address that you can use for online registrations, verifications, and one-time signups without revealing your personal email.
Your temporary inbox will remain active during this session. All emails will be automatically deleted when you close the program.
